New AGOSA Leaders Elected for the 2026/2027 Academic Year

By AGOSA Board · June 2026 · 4 min read

Members across all chapters have voted in a new executive board to lead AGOSA into the 2026/2027 academic year. The election, held during the Mid-Year General Assembly, saw strong turnout from students in Algiers, Constantine, and Oran.

The new board

Prince Charles Otabil — President. Campaigning under the banner "Experience, Dedication & Progress," Otabil brings years of welfare committee experience to the role and has pledged to expand the Emergency Support Fund.

Emmanuel Eli — Vice President. Eli will support the President's agenda while focusing on academic mentorship initiatives across chapters.

Amoasi Frank — Financial Secretary. Frank takes over the books with a promise of full transparency in member contributions and fund allocation.

Badohu M.K Noah — Male Organizer. Running on the "Together as One" campaign, Noah will coordinate events and inter-chapter unity activities throughout the year.

A smooth handover

The outgoing board praised the election process as transparent and well-organized, and has pledged full cooperation during the transition period to ensure continuity in ongoing programs like the New Arrivals Program and Skills & Careers Series.

AGOSA congratulates the newly elected leaders and looks forward to a year of continued unity and progress.
